How it works
01

Post freely

Relethe acts like your personal journal. Post without the weight of permanence. You own your timeline — including the ability to revive anything you choose. No old post can haunt you here.

02

Connect deeply

Relethe's AI superconnector matches you with people worth meeting. Weekly introductions, real conversations, no cold outreach. The feed gives you context on who they are before you ever sit down.

03

Never doomscroll again

Posts age and fade over time. Scroll too deep into old content and Relethe stops you. A message appears — and gets more direct every time you return. A feed built to send you back to your life.
